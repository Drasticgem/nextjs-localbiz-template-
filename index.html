About Scott Group

The longest-established electrical contractor in South Texas

Founded by Chubb Scott in 1920, we've spent over a century powering the Coastal Bend — from knob-and-tube wiring to DCS, solar, and fiber optics. With offices in Corpus Christi, San Antonio, Alice, and Weslaco, no job in South Texas is out of reach.
