# Client Intake (Contractor/Trades)

Collect this before implementation:

## Business Identity

- Legal business name
- Public short name/brand name
- Phone and preferred display format
- Email inbox for leads
- Street address + service area cities
- Business hours

## Services

- Core service categories (3–8)
- Top revenue service
- Emergency/after-hours availability
- Licensing/certification details

## Trust Assets

- Testimonials (name, city, quote)
- Review platform links
- Accreditations, badges, associations
- Team bios and photos

## Conversion

- Primary CTA (Call / Book / Request Estimate)
- Secondary CTA
- Preferred form fields
- Scheduling link (Calendly/Cal.com)

## Media + Brand

- Logo files (SVG preferred)
- Brand colors
- Font preferences
- Project photos/videos

## Compliance + Launch

- Terms/privacy requirements
- Analytics preferences
- Domain and DNS owner
- Launch date target
